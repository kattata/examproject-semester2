As 3 young women living in the present world, we have realized the everyday problems of millions of women. Come, we seem to think about it hardly, in practice we always get the same question „What will I wear?” Sometimes we have the whole morning for it, sometimes we think about it in the shower. However, there are times when there is neither time nor idea. By >>>>>>> origin/master creating Set The Label, we meet such women and we want to help by offering sets of clothes perfect for almost any occasion. Nowadays, it is very important to have at least one top or one pair of pants that match almost everything that will make us feel very comfortable and also confident in our own body.

CREATED WITH PASSION

Set The Label was created as a school project, which in conjunction with passion and commitment turned into the idea of creating a new clothes brand. The project is authored by three girls who together form a great team that, in addition to the usual sale of clothes, wants to offer women something unique. The main assumption of the company is designing sets of clothes that can be easily combined with other clothes that we already have in the closet. In this way, we want to help women save those precious „5 minutes in the morning”, which often prove necessary for us.

EQALITY ABOVE ALL

In addition to creating comfortable and good-looking clothes, the premise of Set The Label is also to get rid of a mistaken vision about the female body. Campaigns of famous brands very often present us with a false image of the female body, which only creates negative thoughts and introduces into complexes. We want to move away from such thinking and present the philosophy that every woman is just as beautiful regardless of her figure. We hope that in this way we can at least slightly increase women’s self-acceptance and strengthen their sense of beauty.

OUR VALUES

QUALITY

At Set The Label, we focus on making our clothes and performing well. We only use the best materials and proven suppliers.

SIMPLICITY

We think that you don’t have to have expensive clothes, accessories or always have a fashionable hairstyle to feel good in your own skin. That is why in our solution we focus on simplicity.

COMMUNITY

One of our company’s most important goals is to create a community where every woman will feel equal, inspired and above all beautiful.

ECO AWARE

The world of business and economics is developing at an amazing pace and we know that economics does not always go in line with ecology. However, here at Set The Label sustainability is definitely more important for us. We are constantly working to ensure that our products are more sustainable, from raw materials to the design and production process.
